Distillation Columns

Introduction

At Yash Engineering Company, we design and manufacture high-performance Distillation Columns engineered for efficient separation of liquid mixtures based on volatility differences. Our columns are widely used in both laboratory-scale and large-scale industrial distillation processes, ensuring reliable and precise fractionation of complex chemical mixtures.

Built with decades of fabrication expertise, our distillation units deliver consistent results in applications such as chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, food & beverage, essential oils, solvents, alcohol production, and other purification processes. Each column is custom-designed to meet exact process parameters including temperature, pressure, vapor-liquid equilibrium, and material compatibility.

Column Internals We Provide

To achieve maximum efficiency, our columns are equipped with precision-engineered internals such as:

  • Bubble cap trays
  • Sieve trays
  • Valve trays
  • Structured packing (metal, SS304/316, PTFE)
  • Random packing (Raschig rings, Pall rings, Saddles)
  • Liquid distributors & redistributors
  • Demister pads
  • Downcomers & weirs

These ensure optimal contact between liquid and vapor, improving separation efficiency.
