Chimney

Introduction

At Yash Engineering Company, we design, manufacture, and supply a wide range of Industrial Chimneys engineered for efficient and safe exhaust of combustion gases from boilers, furnaces, generators, thermic fluid heaters, and industrial processing units. Our chimneys are built to meet stringent environmental, thermal, and structural safety requirements, ensuring reliable performance in both medium-scale and heavy-duty industrial applications.

We specialize in custom-built self-supported chimneys designed specifically for process boilers, guaranteeing optimum draft, stability, and long-term durability. Each chimney is fabricated using high-grade materials and manufactured in compliance with industry standards to handle varying temperature, pressure, and emission conditions.

Self-Supported Chimneys

Ideal for industrial boilers and furnaces, these chimneys are engineered to stand independently without external bracing.
