High-Speed Mixers

Our High-Speed Mixers/Blenders are designed for intensive mixing applications where rapid dispersion, emulsification, or heat-assisted blending is required. These mixers are ideal for combining granules, pigment paste, pigment powder, masterbatch materials, or color concentrates for uniform and consistent output.

The high shear action and heat generated during operation help blend pigment paste and polymer powder effectively, ensuring smooth dispersion and color uniformity.

Applications of High-Speed Mixers
  • Emulsification processes
  • High-shear dispersion
  • Mixing granules with pigment powder or paste
  • Color blending for plastics
  • Processing medium to high viscosity materials
Advantages
  • High mixing efficiency
  • Handles wide viscosity range
  • Fast blending and reduced batch time
  • Excellent balance of flow and shear
  • Suitable for demanding industrial mixing applications

Slow-Speed Mixers

Our Slow-Speed Mixers are engineered for gentle, controlled mixing of low to medium viscosity materials. They feature a single shaft agitator equipped with a wide selection of impeller types to suit functions such as:

  • Let-down (thinning)
  • Blending
  • Tinting
  • Solid suspension
  • Absorption
  • Reaction processes
  • Extraction
  • Heat transfer mixing
  • Holding tanks requiring mild agitation to prevent material settling

These mixers are available in single speed, dual speed, or variable speed configurations, offering enhanced control tailored to specific process requirements.

Advantages of Slow-Speed Mixers
  • Highly efficient for delicate or slow agitation processes
  • Gentle mixing without damaging product integrity
  • Supports continuous operation with low energy consumption
  • Ideal for long-duration blending processes
