Types of Heat Exchangers We Manufacture

1. Fixed Tube Sheet Heat Exchangers

The Fixed Tube Sheet configuration is ideal when there is no requirement for tube bundle removal and the service fluids are non-corrosive or easily cleanable on the shell side. These heat exchangers provide:

  • High mechanical strength
  • Excellent thermal conductivity
  • Cost-effective design
  • Robust construction for high-pressure duties

They are preferred for applications where the process fluid is clean, or the heat exchanger operates in closed-loop systems.

2. U-Tube Heat Exchanger

The U-Tube Type Heat Exchanger is designed for applications where thermal expansion differential between shell and tubes is significant. The U-shaped tube bundle allows for natural expansion, making these units ideal for:

  • Handling high-temperature fluids
  • Services with large thermal variations
  • Situations where tube bundle cleanability is needed on the shell side

The U-tube configuration also enables cost savings due to simpler construction and fewer weld joints.
