Types of Reactors We Manufacture

1. Jacketed Reaction Vessel

The Jacketed Reaction Vessel is ideal where heating or cooling is required using steam, hot oil, or chilled water. The jacket height is typically up to the liquid level of the vessel and can be fabricated either as a single-piece or multi-section jacket, depending on operational requirements.

For higher flow applications, a segmental jacket is preferred — especially when downstream vapors are used as the heating medium. For simpler applications, a plain jacket provides effective and economical heating.

Additionally, spiral baffling can be incorporated inside the jacket to enhance turbulence and increase the heat transfer coefficient, improving overall thermal efficiency.

Key Features:
  • Ideal for controlled heating or cooling processes
  • High thermal efficiency with spiral baffle design
  • Available in single-piece or multi-section configuration
  • Designed for pressure, temperature, and corrosion resistance
2. Limpet Coil Reaction Vessel

The Limpet Reaction Vessel is designed for processes that demand high heat transfer rates, mechanical strength, and operational flexibility. The limpet coil design ensures high velocity and turbulence of the heating or cooling medium, improving heat exchange performance. It also reinforces the vessel wall, providing additional structural rigidity — an essential advantage for high-pressure and high-temperature operations.

To further optimize performance, the limpet coil can be divided into multiple zones, allowing for independent temperature control and enhanced process flexibility.

Key Features:
  • High heat transfer efficiency with turbulent flow
  • Excellent strength and rigidity for high-pressure applications
  • Multiple zone limpet design for temperature flexibility
  • Suitable for both heating and cooling operations

Applications

  • Pharmaceuticals Industry
  • Chemical Industry
  • Paint Industry
