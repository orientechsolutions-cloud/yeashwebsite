Our Story

Yash Engineering Company was founded by Mr. Bapu Krishna Lohar, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep commitment to quality engineering and precision craftsmanship. With his strong technical background and passion for innovation, he built the company on the core values of trust, workmanship, and long-term client satisfaction.

Located in Boisar MIDC, Maharashtra, India, Yash Engineering Company is a leading industrial and construction fabrication company specializing in custom steel fabrication, structural manufacturing, and turnkey project-based engineering solutions. We cater to a wide range of industries including construction, chemical, power, mechanical, and infrastructure development.

With a talented team of 50 - 100 skilled engineers, welders, and fabrication experts, we provide end-to-end services that include:

  • Structural steel fabrication for industrial sheds, platforms, and plant structures
  • Equipment and machinery manufacturing for process and mechanical industries
  • Custom fabrication and installation based on detailed project requirements
  • On-site fabrication, assembly, and maintenance support for industrial projects

At Yash Engineering Company, quality and safety are our top priorities. Every fabrication project is executed with precision, using advanced machinery and high-grade materials that ensure durability and performance. Our Boisar MIDC facility is equipped with modern tools for cutting, welding, and finishing, allowing us to handle both small-scale and large-scale fabrication projects with efficiency.

Over the years, we have built strong partnerships with several local and regional industrial companies, delivering reliable fabrication and engineering services that contribute to India's growing infrastructure and manufacturing ecosystem. Our clients trust us for our on-time delivery, competitive pricing, and commitment to excellence.

As one of the most dependable engineering and steel fabrication companies in Maharashtra, we continuously strive to improve our processes, adopt new technologies, and provide solutions that add real value to every project we undertake.
